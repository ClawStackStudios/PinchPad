# Security Policy 🛡️

## 🔒 Our Commitment

We take the security of PinchPad and its users very seriously. Our core "ShellCryption" and "ClawKeys" systems are designed for maximum privacy and zero-knowledge storage.

## 🛡️ Supported Versions

| Version | Supported |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1.x.x | ✅ Yes |
| < 1.0.0 | ❌ No |

## 🦞 Reporting a Vulnerability

If you've discovered a way to crack the shell, please let us know!

1.  **Don't Post Publicly**: To protect the reef, please don't share details in issues or public forums.
2.  **Private Contact**: Reach out via the secure channels listed in AI Studio.
3.  **Harden the Fix**: We'll work with you to develop a patch and credit you for the catch.

Thank you for helping us stay crusty!
